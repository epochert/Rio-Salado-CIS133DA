Font Families

Font Families, often reffered to as typeface. Here is the usual grouping of generic fonts: serif, sans-serif, cursive, monospace, and fantasy. The differences are serif fonts include the little flags (serifs). Sans-serif fonts are flat ended without the serifs. Cursive fonts mimic handwriten stylings of letting. Fantasy fonts are decorative and playful representations of charachters. Monospace fonts utilize the an identical amount of space for each letter no matter the shape.

Color Methods

The three main ways to indicate color on the web or in text. 1) RGB color utilizes the value of red, green, and blue. Each color is considered a color channel and can be laid on top of others to create unique colors. 2) Hexadecimal colors are represented by codes: letters, numbers, or a combination of two. The numbers range from A to F, and the numbers range to 0 to 9. 3)Color names, simply involve the name of the color, such as red, blue, purple, cayenee red.
